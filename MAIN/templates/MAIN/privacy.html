Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 24, 2025

1. Introduction

BOMBY ("we," "our," or "us") operates bomby.us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Account information: username, email address, password
  • Profile information: bio, profile picture
  • Social media links: YouTube, Twitch, GitHub, Twitter/X, Kick, Instagram
  • Contact form submissions: name, email, subject, message
  • Order and payment information (processed through PayPal)

Information collected automatically:

  • Device information and browser type
  • IP address and location data
  • Pages visited and interactions on our site
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

Information from third parties:

  • Google OAuth: basic profile information (name, email, profile picture) when you sign in with Google

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Provide our streaming setup and web development services
  • Process payments and fulfill orders
  • Display your profile on the promotional wall
  • Communicate with you about orders and services
  • Respond to contact form inquiries
  • Send service-related notifications
  • Improve our website and services
  • Analyze site usage and performance

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

Google Services:

  • Google OAuth for account authentication
  • Google Analytics (gtag.js) for website analytics
  • Google Ads conversion tracking

PayPal:

  • Payment processing for purchases
  • PayPal handles all payment card data; we do not store credit card information

Meta (Facebook):

  • Facebook Pixel for analytics and advertising

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing the use of your data.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you signed in to your account
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze website traffic and usage
  • Support advertising and analytics

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers who assist in operating our website
  • Payment processors (PayPal) to complete transactions
  • Analytics providers to improve our services
  • Law enforcement when required by law

7. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure password hashing
  • Regular security updates

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Export your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at sebetvbusiness@gmail.com.

9. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Order records may be kept for accounting and legal purposes.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us.

11. International Users

BOMBY is operated from the United States. If you access our site from outside the US, your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes by posting a notice on our website.

13. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy or your data:

  • Email: sebetvbusiness@gmail.com
  • Contact Page: bomby.us/contact
